I Am Healthy!

A friendly little weight tracker for iPhone.

What it does Support

Made for everyday use

📝 Quick logging Tap +, type your weight, done. No fluff.
📈 Trend chart See your progress at 1M, 3M, 1Y or all-time.
🎯 Goals + ETA Set a goal weight and get a rough projected date.
❤️ Apple Health Optional one-way sync writes new entries to Health.
👨‍👩‍👧 Multi-person One app for the whole household, separate logs.
⚖️ kg or lb Pick on first launch, switch any time.
🔔 Daily reminder Optional local notification to nudge you.
🔒 On-device Your data stays on your phone. No accounts, no servers.

Privacy in one paragraph

I Am Healthy! stores everything locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData. It does not send your weight, name, or any personal data to a server — there isn't one. If you turn on Apple Health sync for a person, new entries are written to the Health app on your phone using HealthKit; nothing else is shared.

Get started

Open Xcode, run the app, then on first launch:

  1. Pick your preferred unit (kg or lb).
  2. Optionally grant access to Apple Health when prompted.
  3. Tap + on the Log tab to record your first weight.

Living with a partner or tracking the kids' growth? Open Settings → Manage People to add more profiles. Each person gets their own log, color, goal, and trend chart.

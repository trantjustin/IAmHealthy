Privacy

Short version: your data stays on your device.

What we store

I Am Healthy! stores the information you enter — weight entries, optional notes, the names and colors of any people you add, your goal, your unit preference, and your reminder time — locally on your iPhone using Apple's SwiftData framework.

What we don't do

  • No accounts. There is nothing to sign up for.
  • No servers store your weight, name, or any personal data.
  • No advertising IDs, no IDFA, no third-party tracking.

Anonymized usage analytics

I Am Healthy! sends a small number of anonymous usage signals to TelemetryDeck, a privacy-focused analytics service. These signals tell us things like "the app launched", "a weight was logged", or "onboarding finished" — nothing more. We use them only to understand how the app is used in aggregate and to spot bugs.

We never send your weight values, name, date of birth, gender, notes, reminder times, or HealthKit identifiers. TelemetryDeck does not collect your IP address or advertising identifier; it derives a salted, hashed identifier on-device that cannot be reversed to identify you.

See TelemetryDeck's privacy statement for details on what they do and do not store.

Apple Health

If you turn on Sync to Apple Health for a person, new entries you log for them are written to the Health app on your device as Body Mass samples using Apple's HealthKit framework. This data is stored by iOS, not by us, and is governed by Apple's privacy policy and your iOS Health permissions.

I Am Healthy! requests write-only access to body mass — we do not read your Health data.

You can revoke this access at any time in iOS Settings → Health → Data Access & Devices → I Am Healthy!

Notifications

If you enable the daily reminder, the app schedules a local notification on your device. The notification is generated and delivered entirely by iOS — nothing leaves your phone.

Backups

Your I Am Healthy! data is included in your standard iCloud or iTunes/Finder iPhone backups, alongside the rest of your apps. Those backups are managed by Apple, not by us.

Children

The app does not collect any information from anyone, of any age. If you add a child as a "person" in the app, their name and weight entries stay on your device just like yours do.

Changes

If this policy changes in any meaningful way, the updated version will appear at this URL.
